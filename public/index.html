How it works

Three steps to your
first share campaign.

STEP 01
Create your account
Register with a username, password, and your Facebook profile URL. Your account is active the moment you submit — no wait, no approval gate.
STEP 02
Add your cookies
Paste Facebook account cookies into the manager. KiroBoost auto-extracts the EAAG token via the Graph API and validates each cookie before saving.
STEP 03
Launch & track live
Paste your post link, pick a speed mode, set a target share count, and hit start. Every account runs in parallel — watch each share log in real time.
Speed modes

Choose the right pace
for every campaign.

Speed controls the per-account delay between shares. All accounts still run in parallel — speed only changes the cooldown within a single account's loop.

Mode Delay per share Best for Risk
⚡ instant 0 ms — no cooldown Maximum velocity, short bursts Higher
→ fast 2 000 ms Balanced speed, longer campaigns Medium
◌ slow 3 000 ms Sustained runs, aged cookies Low
