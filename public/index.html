Features

Everything you need
to grow on Facebook

From single shares to full batch automation — KiroBoost gives you the tools to scale fast.

Instant Sharing

Fire shares the moment you submit. No queues, no throttling. Delivery starts in under a second with real session cookies.

Batch Engine

Run hundreds of accounts in parallel. Set a target share count and let the engine distribute the workload automatically.

Live Session Monitor

Track every share in real-time with a live log feed. See per-account success and failure counts as they happen.

Cookie Management

Add, validate, and organize your Facebook session cookies. Bulk-delete expired ones. No raw credentials ever stored.

3 Share Methods

Switch between content management, news feed, and business graph API methods to maximize reach and avoid detection.

Speed Control

Choose between Instant, Fast, and Slow modes. Tune the share rate per account to match your safety requirements.

How it works

Up and running
in four steps

No technical setup, no installs. Open your browser and start sharing.

01

Create an account

Register with a username, password, and your Facebook profile link. Activation is instant — no waiting.

02

Add your cookies

Paste Facebook session cookies for each account you want to use. KiroBoost validates them automatically.

03

Paste your post link

Drop in the URL of the Facebook post you want to boost. The engine extracts the post ID in milliseconds.

04

Watch it grow

Hit boost. The live session monitor shows every share as it lands. Stop anytime with a single click.
